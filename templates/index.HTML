About Us

We are all seniors at Sacramento State University and this is our Senior Design Project. Our group consist of two Computer Engineers, Jaishil and Gustavo, and two Electrical and Electronic Engineers, Ciara and Armin. We all love our pets so we wanted to create a door that helps us keep them safe and give us a peace of mind when were are away.

How Our Product Came To Be

The Covid-19 Pandemic has created a unique situation. The Pandemic forced people to stay home with their dogs/cats, allowing pet-owners the ability to spend more time with their animals, which in turn, meant more outside activities for animals that would otherwise be forced to stay inside while their Owners would be off commuting to work. The effect of this is seen in the uptick in Dog and Cat Adoptions/ Veterinary Visits nationwide. As we are entering into a post-Covid era where workers are expected to once again commute back to work, many dog/cat owners are forced to once again leave their pets at home unattended, reducing the quality of life that these sociable animals experienced during the Pandemic. Many dog/cat owners, feeling that they can’t provide the same level of companionship and care they provided during Covid, are actually opting to give their Pets back to shelters, which has only reduced the quality of life for these creatures, as many shelters that were empty during the Pandemic are now overflowing with unwanted dogs/cats, many of which are euthanized as a result. Our team recognizes this Societal Problem as an opportunity to keep Dog/Cat Owners closer to their pets and mitigate the tragedy that comes with kill-shelters, unfavorable wildlife interactions, and run-over pets. It is our expressed hope that by giving Petowners the convenience to monitor their Pet’s coming and goings remotely, that we can provide dogs and cats the freedoms that they got to experience during the Pandemic while also giving Pet-Owners the peace of mind in knowing that their animals are happy and safe.

Who Can Use It

Our door can be used for any pets that are able to be inside or outside.
